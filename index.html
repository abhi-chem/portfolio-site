About

I am a researcher working at the intersection of halide perovskites, photoelectrochemistry, and lithium-ion battery systems. My work focuses on charge transport, interfacial kinetics, and device-level performance enhancement using external stimuli and data-driven approaches.

Research Highlights

Magnetic Field Assisted PEC Water Splitting

Developed novel PEC systems to enhance hydrogen generation efficiency through external magnetic field modulation of charge dynamics.

Halide Perovskite Materials

Designed and synthesized inorganic-organic perovskites to study structure-property relationships and optoelectronic performance.

Lithium-ion Battery R&D

Working on electrolyte formulation, degradation mechanisms, and advanced electrochemical diagnostics in NMC and LFP systems.
