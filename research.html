Research Focus

PEC Water Splitting

Investigating advanced magnetic-field enhanced photoelectrochemical (PEC) systems. By leveraging spin-selective charge transfer mechanisms and morphological tuning, I aim to maximize the solar-to-hydrogen conversion efficiencies of complex oxide interfaces.

Chalcogenide Perovskites

Exploring stable, lead-free alternative perovskite architectures for optoelectronic applications. Focusing on defect engineering and interface passivation to boost the stability and functionality of emerging energy materials.
