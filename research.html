Electrochemistry

Photoelectrochemical Water Splitting

My research investigates the enhancement of solar-to-hydrogen conversion efficiency using external magnetic fields and spin-selective charge transport mechanisms in complex oxide photoelectrodes.

Key findings include the role of magnetic moments in biasing minority carrier extraction at BiVO₄/CoFeOₓ interfaces, leading to a 2× improvement in photocurrent density. This work was published in Journal of Physical Chemistry C (2026).

Materials Science

Chalcogenide & Halide Perovskites

I explore the structural and optoelectronic properties of lead-free chalcogenide perovskites (BaZrS₃) as stable alternatives to halide perovskites for photovoltaic and photocatalytic applications.

Key contributions include elucidating octahedral distortion-induced photoluminescence quenching, bipolaron-mediated hopping conduction, and engineering chalcogenide photoanodes with enhanced charge separation. Published in ChemPhysChem, Dalton Transactions, and ChemPlusChem.
