Battery SoC Prediction

Developed an advanced LSTM-based (Long Short-Term Memory) neural network to predict the State of Charge (SoC) in lithium-ion batteries. This predictive model integrates electrochemical signatures with dynamic time-series data to deliver high-accuracy SoC estimations, critical for Battery Management Systems (BMS) in EVs.
