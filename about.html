Background

I am a PhD-trained Energy Materials Scientist from IIT Madras with a deep focus on the design, synthesis, and characterization of functional materials for energy conversion and storage. My doctoral research spans photoelectrochemical (PEC) water splitting, halide and chalcogenide perovskites, and next-generation battery systems.

At OLA Electric, I work at the intersection of electrochemical R&D and data science, applying machine learning to accelerate the development of safer, longer-lasting lithium-ion batteries for electric vehicles. I am actively seeking postdoctoral opportunities in energy materials and electrochemistry.
